# Target Accounts & Customer Discovery Pipeline

## 1. Ideal Customer Profiles (ICP)

### 1. Boutique MSSPs (Highest Probability)
- **Profile**: SOC-as-a-service boutique, vCISO freelancers, Incident Response (IR) consultants.
- **Pain**: Managing multiple distinct tenants, drowning in repetitive triage, high human cost for low-level alerts.
- **Why RiskLens**: Multi-tenant architecture out-of-the-box. Autonomous playbooks handle Tier-1 triage effortlessly.
- **Scale**: 5–50 clients with no internal SOAR maturity.

### 2. DevOps-Heavy Startups
- **Profile**: Fintech, SaaS infra-heavy, Web3 infra, E-commerce.
- **Pain**: WAF noise, auth anomalies, credential stuffing. Small security/devops team drowning in ops.
- **Why RiskLens**: Perfect wedge for credential stuffing and WAF integrations via Autonomous Playbooks.

### 3. Mid-Size Infra Teams
- **Profile**: Traditional tech companies moving to cloud, compliance-driven teams.
- **Pain**: Need a "SOC-lite" for fast MTTR and compliance evidence.
- **Why RiskLens**: Export layer, immutable audit logs, and AI root cause reconstruction provides instant compliance evidence.
